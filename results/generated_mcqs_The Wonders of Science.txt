## MCQ 1
Question: What is at the heart of scientific inquiry, according to the text?
A) The Power of Technology
B) The Scientific Method
C) The Theory of Relativity
D) The Discovery of Penicillin
Correct Answer: B) The Scientific Method

## MCQ 2
Question: Who is credited with the discovery of penicillin, which revolutionized medicine?
A) Albert Einstein
B) Alexander Fleming
C) Isaac Newton
D) Galileo Galilei
Correct Answer: B) Alexander Fleming

## MCQ 3
Question: What is the result of fields like biology, chemistry, and physics overlapping?
A) Groundbreaking innovations
B) Isolated discoveries
C) Limited understanding
D) Lack of collaboration
Correct Answer: A) Groundbreaking innovations

## MCQ 4
Question: What role do technological advancements play in scientific research?
A) They hinder the progress of science
B) They have no impact on scientific research
C) They play a minor role in scientific research
D) They enhance our ability to explore and understand the universe
Correct Answer: D) They enhance our ability to explore and understand the universe

## MCQ 5
Question: Why is it essential to inspire the next generation of scientists?
A) To reduce the number of scientists
B) To limit the scope of scientific research
C) To cultivate a love for science in young minds
D) To discourage curiosity and critical thinking
Correct Answer: C) To cultivate a love for science in young minds
